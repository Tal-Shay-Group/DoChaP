what can you search for in DoChaP?
-for genes - by their GeneID, name (symbol name), synonyms and will later support different IDs
-for transcripts - by their transcript RefSeq ID
-for proteins - by their RefSeq ID
-if you don't know what to search for try the result example in the navigation bar or by clicking here

what can DoChaP help you with?
-finding domain locations in proteins
-finding exon locations in genes
-comparing transcripts
-understanding about possible outcomes of alternative splicing
